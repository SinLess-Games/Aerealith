## Permitted References

Truthful, non-confusing nominative references are permitted, including:

- "Compatible with Aerealith"
- "Built using Aerealith"
- "Unofficial Aerealith integration"

Use only what is reasonably necessary to identify compatibility or origin.
Clearly identify your own product, organization, and distribution, and state
that it is unofficial when confusion is possible.

## Uses Requiring Permission

Without prior written permission, do not:

- impersonate the official project, company, maintainers, or services;
- suggest sponsorship, certification, affiliation, or endorsement;
- name a fork or competing hosted service "Aerealith" or "Aerealith AI";
- use official logos, mascots, icons, or badges as another product's primary
  identity;
- misrepresent unofficial or modified builds as official releases;
- create confusing domains, accounts, package names, or distribution names; or
- alter or combine official marks in a way that creates a confusing new mark.
