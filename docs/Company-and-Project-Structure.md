# Company and Project Structure

Status: Active
Owner: SinLess Games LLC
Last Updated: 2026-07-18
Document Type: Organization and Naming Standard
Authority: Canonical company, product, and public-naming relationship

## Purpose

This document defines how SinLess Industries, SinLess Games LLC, Aerealith,
and Aerealith AI relate to one another. Use it for documentation, product copy,
contributor attribution, funding material, contracts, policies, and technical
references.

It distinguishes organizational operation from legal ownership. The repository
establishes SinLess Games LLC as the current legal party, while SinLess
Industries is the operating umbrella.

## Canonical Hierarchy

```text
SinLess Industries
`-- SinLess Games LLC
    `-- Aerealith
        `-- Aerealith AI
```

This is an organizational and product hierarchy. It does not, by itself,
establish the legal form of SinLess Industries or transfer any asset, contract,
or intellectual-property right.

## Roles

### SinLess Industries

SinLess Industries is the operating parent and organizational umbrella for
SinLess Games LLC and the Aerealith project. SinLess Games and Aerealith AI are
operated under SinLess Industries.

The repository does not establish whether SinLess Industries is a separately
incorporated legal entity. Until corporate records say otherwise, do not name
SinLess Industries as the contracting party, copyright holder, payment
recipient, trademark owner, or data controller solely because it is the
operating umbrella.

### SinLess Games LLC

SinLess Games LLC is the legal company and development studio currently named
throughout the repository's policies, copyright notices, payment language, and
contracting material. It develops and publishes Aerealith under the SinLess
Industries operating umbrella.

Unless verified legal records are updated, use SinLess Games LLC for:

- Contracts and signed agreements.
- Billing, crowdfunding, grants, and investment transactions.
- Copyright, licenses, and intellectual-property notices.
- Privacy, data-processing, security, and legal policies.
- Domain, trademark, and regulatory registrations.
- Formal support, enforcement, and compliance actions.

Use **SinLess Games** only as informal shorthand. Use **SinLess Games LLC** in every
legal or ownership context.

### Aerealith

Aerealith is the product and platform. It connects applications, communities,
workflows, automation, data, and intelligent capabilities through a modular,
user-controlled system.

Public product messaging should call the platform **Aerealith**. Technical and
product documents must not use Aerealith AI as a synonym for the entire
platform.

### Aerealith AI

Aerealith AI is the assistant and intelligence layer within Aerealith. It helps
users understand connected systems, review recommendations, coordinate
workflows, and perform explicitly authorized actions.

Aerealith AI is operated under SinLess Industries and delivered through the
Aerealith platform by SinLess Games LLC. It is a capability of Aerealith, not a
separate company and not the name of the whole platform.

| Context                                     | Required name or wording                                                                  |

| Parent or operating umbrella                | SinLess Industries                                                                        |
| Legal company, studio, or contracting party | SinLess Games LLC                                                                         |
| Informal studio reference                   | SinLess Games                                                                             |
| Product, platform, website, or repository   | Aerealith                                                                                 |
| Assistant or intelligence layer             | Aerealith AI                                                                              |
| Public ownership line                       | Aerealith is developed by SinLess Games LLC and operated under SinLess Industries.        |
| Contributor attribution                     | Contributing to Aerealith, a SinLess Games LLC product operated under SinLess Industries. |
| Copyright                                   | Copyright SinLess Games LLC, unless verified legal records identify another owner.        |
| Legal policies and agreements               | SinLess Games LLC, until counsel-approved documents identify another legal party.         |

Technical identifiers such as the `@aerealith-ai/*` package scope may retain
their existing spelling. They are implementation identifiers, not guidance for
public product naming.

## Contributor and Community Language

Contributors work on Aerealith. Contribution does not imply employment,
partnership, agency, authority to bind SinLess Industries or SinLess Games LLC,
or ownership of the project.

Preferred attribution:

> Contributing to Aerealith, a SinLess Games LLC product operated under SinLess
> Industries.

Community members, moderators, and third-party developers should use the same
product distinction: Aerealith is the platform; Aerealith AI is its assistant.

## Future SinLess Industries Projects

SinLess Industries may operate additional studios, products, services, or
projects in the future. A shared operating umbrella does not automatically make
those projects part of Aerealith.

Future projects may share approved organizational capabilities, infrastructure,
or standards, but they must have explicit boundaries for:

- Legal ownership and contracting.
- Branding and product names.
- User identity and authorization.
- Data access, retention, and deletion.
- Infrastructure and operational responsibility.
- Funding, accounting, and licensing.

No future project receives access to Aerealith data, permissions, accounts, or
authority merely because both are operated under SinLess Industries.

## Legal and Organizational Caveat

This repository is not a substitute for formation documents, operating
agreements, trademark records, copyright assignments, tax records, or legal
advice. The current repository evidence names SinLess Games LLC as the legal
party. Any change to legal ownership or responsibility must first be confirmed
in the relevant records and reviewed by qualified counsel, then reflected in
policies, contracts, funding pages, domains, and copyright notices.
